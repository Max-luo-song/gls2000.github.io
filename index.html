Biography

I am currently a PhD student jointly trained by East China Normal University(ECNU) and Shanghai Innovation Institute(SII), supervised by Prof. Yuan Xie. I received my Master degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) in 2025, supervised by Prof. Kun Zhu. Prior to that, I completed my Bachelor's degree in the same school at NUAA in 2022.

My research interests include Embodied 3D Spatial Intelligence, Trusted Multi-modal Large Model. Specifically, I work on

  • Perception and Understanding of 3D

  • Visual Language Navigation

  • Continue Learning of Large models
